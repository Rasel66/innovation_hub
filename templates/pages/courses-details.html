Power BI: Data Visualization & Business Intelligence

This course will teach you how to turn raw data into meaningful insights using Microsoft Power BI. You will learn to connect to data sources, transform data, create interactive dashboards, and generate reports that help make informed business decisions. The course is practical, hands-on, and suitable for beginners as well as professionals looking to enhance their analytical skills.
